TRAINING COURSES

Universal Development Center organizes Web Programming classes.
The purpose of the course is to teach students to create ordinary and animated web sites of any complexity, install them on the internet, make information updates, optimize sites for successful indexing in search engines.
The duration of the courses is 5 months, 3 times per week, for 2 hours a day.
The first lesson is FREE.
The trainings are conducted in three stages:
1st stage (HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap) -25,000 AMD
2nd (JavaScript, JQuery) -30,000 AMD
3rd (PHP, MSQL, OOP) -35,000 AMD

This is the entry-level of our Illustrator classes, and introduces you to the powerful Pen tool, discover how to transform existing shapes, and learn how to add color to drawn objects.
The best students will be given the opportunity to practice and work in our center.
The Duration of the courses is 5 months ,3 times per week for 2 hours (35 000 AMD / month).
